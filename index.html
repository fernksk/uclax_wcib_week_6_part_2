OUR WATER

We offers one of the softest artesian waters in the world, rich in minerals like silica which are revitalising for the skin, a balanced alkalinity, which provides a neutral or “sweet” taste and low levels of nitrates which make it one of the purest and cleanest waters in the world.

 

SUSTAINABILITY

Since 2017, MUVRA's bottling site and all water sold in North America has been certified Carbon Neutral by "The Carbon Trust". Today, we’re proud to announce that the brand has achieved Carbon Neutral certification globally.

Using recycled PET results in 50% lower greenhouse gas emissions compared to using virgin PET for packaging production. We also partner up with “TerraCycle®” to provide recycle and return bottle stations across United States.

 

We will continue to optimize to maintain our carbon neutral status each year, while continue to innovate, preserve our water ecosystem and work with local communities.
